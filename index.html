The Beatles were an English rock and roll band formed in Liverpool, England in 1960. The group was made up of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, Ringo Starr, and George Harrison. This iconic group is known for transforming popular culture and music over the decades that they released albums. They are, to this day, regarded as one of the most influential bands of all time.
